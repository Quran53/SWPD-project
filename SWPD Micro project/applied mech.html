APPLIED MECHANICS

Program Details

Applied Mechanics Department of this institute is very much rich in facilities and laboratories facilities. Faculties and supporting staffs are completely dedicated towards academic responsibilities as well as committed for the progress of the institute.

Applied Mechanics Laboratory is mainly divided in four major catogories i.e. (1) Applied Mechanics Lab. (2) Strength of Material Lab. (3) Soil Engineering Lab. And (4) Concret Technology Lab. All the labs are equipped with required precise and modern equipments like UTM-200 KN and Digital Compression Testing Machine-2000 KN capacity.

With these facilities Department is not only capable to perform all the practicals but also doing Material Testing work and Consultancy work and giving excellent contribution for revenue generation.

All these put the Students very much aligned with today's needs for Modern Construction Indutry and helpful for bright carrier.
