CIVIL ENGINEERING

Program Details

The Department of Civil Engineering of this Institute started in 1959 with this Polytechnic with an intake capacity of 30 students which has been increased to 180.

PROGRAM OFFERS 3 YEARS DIPLOMA COURSE ON SEMESTER BASED PATTERN.

TEACHING METHODOLOGY:AWARD OF DEGREE: Diploma in Civil Engineering will be awarded by Gujarat Technological University on sucessful completion of 3 years program.

VISION:

To promote competent human resourse by providing quality and latest technical education for fulfilling changing societal needs in the area of Civil Engineering.

MISSION:

1) To promote quality education as per industrial and social needs.

2) To provide knowledge of various Civil Engineering aspects and skills based practices.

3) To make students aware of latest development in the field and make them sustain against challenges by inculcating technical competence as well as ethical values.

4) To motivate students to be entrepreneurs.

LABS

COMPUTER LAB

This Laboratory is useful to understand the basic
programs such as CAD and CAG to students for enhancing their
skills in drawing.

PHE LAB

This Laboratory is useful to understand the basic
tests conducted on water such as PH value, Alkalinity, BOD and
COD test as well as helpful for various test conducted on
Basic Transportation Engineering to determine properties of Bitumen.

SURVEYING LAB

This Laboratory is useful to understand the various
practicals in suvey and advanced survey lab as chain surveying,
Tacheometric survey, Plane table survey, GPS etc.
