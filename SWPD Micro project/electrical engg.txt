  <h1 div class="center"> ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING </h1>
  <h2 div class="center"> Program Details</h2>
  <p  div class="center"><b>Program Duration:</b> 3 Years<br></p>
   <h5 div class="center">VISION:</h5>
  <p div class="center"> To impart quality education in Electrical Engineering<br>
           	     to meet industrial and social needs.<br></p>
   <h5 div class="center">MISSION:</h5>
   <p div class="center"> By inspiring students to develop skills in Electrical<br>
                     Engineering field as per industrial and social needs.<br>
                     By developing  ethics, moral, and value based professionalism.<br>
                     By motivating young minds to acquire and updated<br>
                     knowledge continuosly and learn to apply it.</p>
  <h5 div class="center"><u>Program Educational Objectives(PEOs):</u></h5><br>
   <p div class="center"><b><u>PEO-1:</u></b> Improve/develop the Electrical Engineering systems<br>
                           that are technically sounded, economical and socially<br>
                           environmental acceptable.<br>
      <b><u>PEO-2:</u></b> Exhibit professionalism, ethical attitude, team spirit &<br>
                           persue lifelong learning to achieve career and organizational<br>
                           goals.<br>
      <b><u>PEO-3:</u></b> Provide sound theoretical and practical knowledge of Electrical<br>
                           Engineering to develop managerial and enterpreneurial skills<br>
                           which enable students to contribute to the well-fare of society.<br></p>
  <h5 div class="center"><u>Program Specific Outcomes(PSOs):</u></h5><br>
   <p div class="center"><b><u>PSO-1:</u></b> Identify the electrical problems and trouble shoot them<br>
                           by applying principles and fundamentals of Electrical<br>
                           Engineering.<br>
      <b><u>PSO-2:</u></b> To test, operate, supervise and maintain the electrical<br>
                           system.<br></p>
