    From the Desk of a Principal

    Greetings! Welcome to Government
    Polytechnic Dahod.

    "Education empowers people with the skills,
    knowledge, and values they need to build a better world."



    Government Polytechnic, Dahod is one of the centers
    of quality education in the field of technical education
    in Gujarat.
    The college was established in the year 1959 and has been
    functioning well since then.The college offers Diploma Engineering degrees
    in 6 branches. At the end of successful completion of all the semesters a
    student is awarded a Diploma Engineering degree by Gujarat Technological
    University, Ahmedabad-one of the forefront universities in Gujarat.



    The institute also offers separates hostel facility for boys and girls
    students of the college, where they can adjust very soon due to homely
    atmosphere. If you are inspiring a bright futures of yours, this institute
    is meant just for you!.The institute welcomes all the Diploma Engineering
    aspirants students of Gujarat who are waiting to create their successful
    career and future ahead.
